The text on this cornerstone is sans-serif and there are a variety weight in the strokes. This is most prominent in the tail of the number 9 as well as the bottom of the curves in the numbers 3 and 5. The angled terminals on the 3 and 5 add dimension to the cornerstone while also creating a contrast with the curvature. This cornerstone is on the side of a building that is curently a fire station. Despite this, the composition and forms of this text are well-designed and thought out.
